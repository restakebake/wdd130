Methodology

I use the Least Intrusive, Minimally Aversive (LIMA) technique training. LIMA uses the least intrusive, minimally aversive strategy out of a set of humane and effective tactics likely to succeed in achieving a training or behavior change objective. LIMA does not justify the use of punishment in lieu of other effective interventions and strategies.
